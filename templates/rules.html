🎓 Welcome to ATU Stack, the Uni Resource Game!

Earn as many 🎓 Credits as possible!
Spend resources to complete tasks
Beware of the 🤢 Hangovers

🧍 Starting Resources

  • 🍕 Pizza: 3
  • ☕ Coffee: 3
  • 😴 Sleep: 3
  • 📖 Study: 3

⚙️ Actions

1. ✅ Collect Resource

Click to collect some random resources (pizza, coffee, sleep, or study).

☠️ Hangover Risk:
Each collect increases your chance of a hangover event, which steals half of all your resources (but not credits).

Hangover chances:
  • 1st collect: 0%
  • 2nd: 5%
  • 3rd: 10%
  • 4th: 15%
  • 5th: 20%
  • 6th and beyond: 25% max

2. 📚 Submit Task

Spend resources to earn 🎓 Credits!

Task Cost Reward
CA Quiz 1 📖 Study, 1 😴 Sleep 1 🎓 Credits
Assignment 1 🍕 Pizza, 2 📖 Study, 1 ☕ Coffee 2 🎓 Credits
Exam 2 🍕 Pizza, 2 📖 Study, 2 ☕ Coffee, 2 😴 Sleep 5 🎓 Credits

3. 🔄 Trade

Offer resources to others and accept trades. Great way to balance your inventory!

4. 📊 View Leaderboard

See who’s winning the academic arms race in real time.

🛠️ Admin Controls

  • ⏱️ Set game start/end time
  • 🧹 Reset game
  • 📜 View logs (actions, trades, hangovers)
